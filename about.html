About Cascades Procurement

Two partners. One mission: helping you get exactly what you need, at a price that's fair.

Our Story

Built on Hustle, Honesty, and a Handshake

Cascades Procurement started with a simple idea: buying and sourcing vehicles and equipment shouldn't feel like a gamble. Too many people overpay, settle for the wrong fit, or waste weeks chasing dead-end listings.

We're two business partners who saw that gap and decided to fill it. Between us, we've spent years studying markets, building supplier relationships, and learning how to spot the deals worth making — and the ones worth walking away from.

Whether you're a contractor who needs a reliable excavator, a small business expanding its fleet, or someone looking for the right personal vehicle, we treat every sourcing request with the same care. Your goals become our goals.

How We Work

Simple, Transparent, Effective

We keep our process straightforward because finding the right deal should never feel complicated.

01

Tell Us What You Need

We start with a conversation. What are you looking for? What's your budget? What matters most — price, condition, timeline? We listen first.

02

We Search & Evaluate

We tap into our network and the broader market to find options that fit your criteria. Every option gets vetted for value, reliability, and fit.

03

We Negotiate & Advise

Once we've found strong matches, we negotiate on your behalf and give you an honest assessment. If something isn't worth it, we'll say so.

04

You Decide

We present our findings, you make the call. No pressure, no hard sell. Just the information you need to make a confident decision.
